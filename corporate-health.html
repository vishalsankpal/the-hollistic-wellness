First Aid Training

We help organizations empower their employees towards more Healthier lifestyles and choices. We have worked with Marquee organizations in the areas of Employee Health Screening, Health Risk analysis, First Aid & EmergencyResponse and Counseling & Awareness. The Emergency Response / First Aid Training (ERT) program will constitute a workshop comprising of Theory and Practical demonstration. We endeavor to make Schools a Safer place by providing Emergency Response and First Aid Training. Providing eﬀective First Aid can make the diﬀerence between life and death, rapid v/s prolonged recovery and temporary v/s permanent disability. We have worked with multitude of K-12 Schools and Pre-primary centers in training their staﬀ, students and members of PTA in the area of First Aid. Our services are tailored for Teachers, Support Staﬀ, Students and Parents.
